Welcome to the Veggie Crew, where we are passionate about cooking and serving delicious, nutritious vegan meals! 🥦 Our team is dedicated to creating a wide variety of healthy dishes for vegetarians and co-opers, ensuring that every meal is 100% plant-based. This means absolutely no ingredients related to animals—just pure, wholesome goodness from nature. 🌿

Our goal is to offer more diverse and satisfying options for those embracing a plant-based lifestyle 🍃, while maintaining the highest standards of taste and health. 💚

We work in two shifts daily 🕒, each lasting 3 hours to ensure fresh meals at the right time:

Morning Shift: 8:00 AM – 11:00 AM ☀️

Afternoon Shift: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM 🌅

Join us in our mission to make healthy eating easy, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone! 💪🍽️
